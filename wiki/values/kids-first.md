## Statement

Ford's children's wellbeing is a top-order filter on major life decisions — including where to live. He prioritizes strong public schools at every level and environments safe enough for free-range childhood.

## Evidence

- **2026-04-22 voice memo**: "Good public schools — that's a huge one — good public schools for elementary, middle, and high school… a real community where I can let my kids free range."

## Implications

- School quality is a hard requirement (not a nice-to-have) in the home-buying search.
- "Free-range" framing signals Ford values autonomy and outdoor independence in childhood — consistent with an active, outdoors-oriented personal lifestyle.
- This value likely interacts with the co-parenting situation (Bridget) and custody logistics.

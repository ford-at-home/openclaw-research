## Statement

Ford wants to be surrounded by people who are energetic, healthy, and excited to be alive. He actively avoids environments dominated by depression or low vitality, and he prizes communities where care and investment flow in both directions.

## Evidence

- **2026-04-22 voice memo**: "I really care for neighborhoods that I'm in an area that has people that are energetic and excited to be alive and not depressed and unhealthy… people with resources and able to like take care of me just as much as I take care of them."

## Implications

- This value is a primary filter in his home-buying search — neighborhood vibe ranks alongside practical criteria like schools.
- The reciprocity framing suggests Ford is a high-contributor in social/community contexts and expects mutual investment, not just passive proximity.

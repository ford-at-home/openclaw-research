## Risk Mitigation & The "Family Inspection" Checklist

Historic charm comes with historic hazards. Families renting in Richmond's most walkable, older neighborhoods must prioritize environmental safety.

### Environmental & Structural Risks
The primary source of lead hazards among young children is paint used in buildings built before 1978 [8]. Given that neighborhoods like The Fan and Church Hill are predominantly historic, this is a critical inspection point. Approximately 16,000 children in Richmond are at risk of being impacted by lead poisoning [9].
